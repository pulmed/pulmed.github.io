We use bulk RNA-seq and expression deconvolution approaches to profile molecular programs in pulmonary disease cohorts and intervention studies.

## Probe Based

We apply probe-based transcriptomic platforms, including microarrays and NanoString, for robust targeted and genome-scale expression profiling across cohorts.
