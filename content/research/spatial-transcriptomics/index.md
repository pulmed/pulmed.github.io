---
title: Spatial Transcriptomics
date: 2026-05-01
summary: Spatially resolved gene expression in intact tissue architecture.
---

Spatial transcriptomics adds tissue context to expression data, enabling analysis of niche-specific programs and cell-cell communication in pulmonary disease.

## Sequence-based

Sequence-based spatial transcriptomics is used for transcriptome-wide profiling with preserved spatial coordinates.

## Image-based

Image-based spatial approaches enable targeted high-resolution mapping of RNA signals directly in tissue architecture.
