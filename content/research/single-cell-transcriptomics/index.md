Single-cell transcriptomics allows us to resolve cellular heterogeneity, identify rare cell states, and map immune-stromal interactions in lung tissue.

## sc/snRNA

We use single-cell and single-nucleus RNA sequencing (sc/snRNA) to profile cell states across tissues and disease conditions.

## TCR-seq

TCR-seq is used to characterize T-cell clonality, expansion, and repertoire dynamics.

## CITE-seq

CITE-seq integrates transcriptomic and surface protein information for joint cell-state and phenotype analysis.
