We investigate DNA methylation and chromatin-associated regulatory processes that influence pulmonary disease trajectories and treatment sensitivity.

## ATAC

ATAC-based profiling is used to quantify chromatin accessibility and identify active regulatory elements.

## ChIP

ChIP approaches are applied to map histone marks and transcription factor occupancy across relevant loci.

## Hi-C

Hi-C is used to study 3D genome organization and long-range regulatory interactions.
