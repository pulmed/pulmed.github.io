Our genomics research focuses on identifying germline and somatic variants associated with pulmonary phenotypes, treatment response, and disease progression.

## WES/WGS

We use whole-exome sequencing (WES) and whole-genome sequencing (WGS) to detect coding and non-coding variants relevant to pulmonary disease biology.

## GWAS

Genome-wide association studies (GWAS) are used to identify loci associated with disease susceptibility, severity, and treatment outcomes.

## PRS

Polygenic risk scores (PRS) are evaluated to quantify inherited risk and improve stratification of patient subgroups.
