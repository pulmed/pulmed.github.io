Our proteomics analyses quantify protein abundance and post-translational patterns to reveal functional mechanisms and candidate clinical biomarkers.

## Spectral flow cytometry

We use spectral flow cytometry to characterize immune and stromal cell phenotypes at high dimensionality.

## IMC (Hyperion)

Imaging mass cytometry (IMC, Hyperion) is used for multiplexed protein mapping with preserved tissue context.

## Immunofluorescence

Immunofluorescence supports spatial validation of protein expression and colocalization patterns in tissue sections.

## Immunohistochemistry

Immunohistochemistry is used for targeted protein detection and pathology-oriented interpretation in translational settings.
